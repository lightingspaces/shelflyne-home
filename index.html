Brilliant LED lighting for joinery made easy

Shelflyne Home's revolutionary patented design makes it easier than ever to install linear LED strip lighting into joinery. It's quick, affordable and ensures a premium finish every time, without the hassle.

Simple to install with no wires or soldering

Light up any shelving system quickly and affordably with the wireless and solder-free LED technology. Our patented FLIP design eliminates polarity issues. Regardless of how the profile is mounted the Light Profile will light up. Simple and easy not just for the end user, but also for installers.

A premium finish without the hassle

A streamlined time-saving installation process translates to cost savings and zero hassle for installers. Simply route the specified channel depth and width for each component and push in tightly to complete. We've done the hard work so you can concentrate on more important things.

Flexible and seamless modular design

Shelflyne Home's modular approach allows the light profile to be mounted in either a downlighting, uplighting or side-lighting configuration. Now you can illuminate any space beautifully, and seamlessly.

Available in a range of convenient sizes

Need some custom sizing for your project? Talk to us today.
